Fresh Veggie Salad

A simple and healthy salad for daily diet.

Ingredients

  • 🥒 Cucumber
  • 🍅 Tomato
  • 🥕 Carrot
  • 🥬 Lettuce
  • 🧂 Salt & Pepper
  • 🍋 Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Wash all vegetables
  2. Cut into small pieces
  3. Mix in a bowl
  4. Add salt, pepper, lemon juice
  5. Serve fresh
