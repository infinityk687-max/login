🥦 Clean Your Food

How to wash fruits & vegetables properly

💧 Rinse with Water

Wash under clean running water.

🖐️ Rub Gently

Remove dirt using your hands.

🧂 Use Salt Water

Helps remove pesticides.

🧼 Peel if Needed

Remove outer skin if required.

🧺 Dry Properly

Use clean cloth or air dry.
